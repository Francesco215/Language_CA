Self-organizing text

Here we take the idea of self organizing systems and apply it to text

In Cellular automata neighboring cells communicate between each other in order to organize with remarcable accuracy large scale scructures with high detail at a small scale

Language models have proven unreliable at long distances due to the limited input size of the transformer. If it were possible to create a model that makes neighboring pieces of text like a CA we should expect to have a final result where the output is coherent at long distances.

The model

A single cell here can be a token, a phrase or in general a piece of text with some extra hidden channel that can be used to communicate information that is not explicitly written in the text.

At each step the model updates a cell based on its internal state and the state of its surrounding cells, and the function is iterated for some steps
